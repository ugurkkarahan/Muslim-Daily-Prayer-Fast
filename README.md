# Muslim Daily: Prayer & Fast

iOS app for Ramadan. Displays iftar and sahur times, countdown, daily prayer (dua), and reminder notifications.

## Features

- **Iftar & Sahur Times** — Real-time prayer times based on your selected location
- **Live Countdown** — Time remaining until iftar, sahur, or Ramadan start
- **Daily Dua** — A different prayer each day
- **Notifications** — Reminders 2 hours, 1 hour, and 30 minutes before iftar and sahur; greeting at iftar time; daily prayer reminder at 12:00 PM
- **Theme** — Light, dark, or system theme
- **Multi-language** — English, Turkish, Hindu and Arabic (Localizable)
