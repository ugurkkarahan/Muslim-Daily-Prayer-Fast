# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

Muslim Daily: Prayer & Fast ("the app") respects your privacy. This policy describes how the app collects, uses, and protects your data.

## 1. Data Collected

### 1.1 Data You Provide

- **Location/Address:** You manually select a city or district name within the app. This address is used to calculate iftar and sahur times. The address is stored only on your device (UserDefaults) and is sent to the prayer times service (Aladhan API) when needed.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Data

- **Device Information:** The advertising service (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, and usage data.
- **Advertising Data:** AdMob may collect data to improve the advertising experience. On iOS 14.5+ devices, "App Tracking Transparency" (ATT) permission is requested; you may decline this permission.

### 1.3 Data Stored on Device (UserDefaults)

- Selected location/address
- Theme preference (light/dark/system)
- Onboarding completion status

This data is stored only on your device and is not shared with third parties.

## 2. Use of Data

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Address | Calculating iftar and sahur times (Aladhan API) |
| Advertising ID | Personalized ads (if you consent) |
| Notification permission | Iftar, sahur, and daily prayer reminders |

## 3. Third-Party Services

### 3.1 Google AdMob

The app uses Google AdMob. Banner and App Open ads are displayed. AdMob's data collection policy: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 3.2 Aladhan API

Prayer times are calculated via [Aladhan API](https://aladhan.com/). Your selected address is sent to this service. Aladhan's privacy policy: [Aladhan API](https://aladhan.com/).

### 3.3 Apple MapKit

Location search uses Apple's MapKit (MKLocalSearchCompleter) service. Search queries are sent to Apple.

## 4. Notifications

The app sends local notifications:

- 2 hours, 1 hour, and 30 minutes before iftar
- 2 hours, 1 hour, and 30 minutes before sahur
- Greeting at iftar time ("May God Accept")
- Daily prayer reminder at 12:00 PM

Notification permission is optional; you can disable it in system Settings.

## 5. Data Security

- Address and preferences are stored unencrypted in UserDefaults on your device.
- Advertising data is processed on Google's servers.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from users under 13 years of age.

## 7. Changes

When this policy is updated, changes will be reflected on this page. We may send in-app notifications for significant changes.
